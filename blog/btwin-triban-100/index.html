Review of Decathlon BTWIN Triban 100

Owned from January 2018 to June 2019

Nazim
Aug 9, 2020 Blog
Bike Specs

BTWIN Triban 100 bought from the Decathlon store at Bedok, Singapore was my first drop-bar road bike. This is the most economical and perfect bike for new cyclists starting to ride on the roads and to decide if

road.cc had a detailed review of bike here which helped immensely in the decision to buy this bike.

Another thread on the bikeradar forum also helped me to decide on some of the upgrades for the bike. One of the most useful upgrade was to replace the tyres which came on the bike to something lighter and the replacing the decathlon brake pads with Kool Stop brake pads.

Pros:

  • Price - A lot of bike for very little money!
  • Beginner Friendly
  • Stable handling

Cons:

  • Index shifter needs getting used to
  • Struggles to climb
  • Size S came only with one water bottle mount

Total Kilometers Used: 3,244.50 KMs / 2016.03 Miles

Total Cost: SGD 300.00

The seat cover was removed once I bought cycling bibs.

Below details are taken from Decathlon’s website and used here to provide the specifications of the bike I used.

Component Description
Frame Comfort-oriented frame geometry with a higher steering socket and a shorter top tube that offers a more upright position than a classic road frame. 6061 T6 aluminium sloping frame with semi-integrated headset. Frame weight, size M: 1830g.
Fork Straight steel fork for greater comfort and precision. Aheadset 1"1/8 head tube that provides great strength and rigidity to the steering assembly.
Handlebar/Stem Aluminium handlebars Handlebar width: 420mm Aluminium stem Length: XS: 90mm S: 90mm M: 90mm L: 110mm XL: 110mm
Drivetrain Indexed speed changes using 7-speed Shimano A050 shifter on the handlebar. Ergonomic and very comfortable: change speeds without letting go of the top part of the handlebar. This ergonomically-shaped lever fits the palm of your hand perfectly. Shimano Tourney 7-speed rear derailleur.
Crankset/Cassette Single chainring is easier to use and reduces weight significantly: 48 teeth.Cranks: 170mm.Cassette: Shimano 14 x 34. Inspired by mountain bikes, the 34 Mega Range helps you manage your effort.
Brakes Tektro RL 340 brake levers offering good grip and excellent rigidity.
Wheels Double-walled wheels. Quick release attachment (9mm) at the front for easy manoeuvring and transport. Nut mount for the rear wheel.
Tyres Hybrid sport 700 x 32 tyres.The tread has small studs for increased performance. The sides of the tyres have larger studs for better grip in bends on consolidated or loose ground and on gravel.
Saddle / Seat Post 100 Sport Saddle Black Aluminium seat post. Diameter: 27.2mm for added comfort thanks to its flexibility Length: 250mm on XS / S 350mm on M / L / XL
Pedals Hybrid platform pedals (good grip for use on trails).
Accessories Comes with a front and rear-lighting kit and bell.
Weight 11.300kg in size M, without pedals.
Composition Frame (for bike) : 100.0% Aluminium 6061.
Warranty Lifetime warranty on manufacturing defects for all metal frames, rigid forks, stems & handlebars 5 years warranty on all carbon frames & forks 2 years warranty on all other parts Excludes damages arising from normal wear & tear, misuse, negligence and unauthorized modifications.
