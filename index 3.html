Biography

Nazim is a PRINCE2 Agile Practitioner in Agile Project Management with 14+ years of experience as a SAP HCM Consultant. Nazim is also a SAP Certified BW Data Modelling and Administration Consultant.

Experienced in working with both Private & Public sector clients, with multiple HCM implementations completed successfully. Experienced in functional and technical testing of HR modules like PA, OM, Benefits, Payroll, Travel Management, Time Management. Have worked closely with development teams as a functional consultant to build test cases and test plan for the implementation projects. Have used tools to create automated test cases in SAP Solution manager and tools like JIRA and HPQC to record test cases and outcomes.

Proficient in data migration & conversion from legacy to SAP Systems, Object Oriented ABAP, Logical Databases (PNPCE), ABAP Web Dynpros, Fiori, SuccessFactors, License Administration, Talent management and appraisals and all submodules of SAP HCM. Proven ability to acquire skills in emerging technologies. Skilled in Analysis of problems & root causes, developing procedures and finding innovative solutions.
